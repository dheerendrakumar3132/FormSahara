Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries before you contact us.

Does Form Sahara charge any fees?

No. Form Sahara is a 100% free informational directory. We do not charge citizens for accessing links. Any payment required for a government service is paid directly on the official government website.

Can you fill out my form for me?

Yes, absolutely! We provide a premium online form-filling service. Our experts can fill out government, scholarship, admission, and job application forms on your behalf for a nominal processing fee. Simply reach out to us on WhatsApp with your details, and we will handle the rest.

A link is not working, what should I do?

Government servers can sometimes experience downtime due to heavy traffic. Please wait a few hours and try again. If the issue persists, kindly select "Report a Broken Link" in our contact form above.

Are you affiliated with the Government?

No, we are an independent, private informational portal built to help citizens. We are not affiliated with the Government of India or any state authorities.
