Who We Are

Navigating government websites and finding the right application form can often feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. Form Sahara was born out of a simple necessity: to bring clarity, organization, and ease to the citizens of India who are looking to access public services.

Based out of Naubasta, Kanpur Nagar (UP), we are a dedicated informational portal that curates, categorizes, and provides direct, safe links to official government portals. Whether you are a student applying for a scholarship, a farmer checking PM Kisan installments, or a senior citizen applying for a pension, Form Sahara is designed to guide you to the exact official page you need, without the confusion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the digital divide by simplifying e-governance for the common man. We strive to provide an ad-free, clutter-free, and straightforward directory of government services, ensuring that vital resources are just a click away for everyone, regardless of their technical expertise.

Our Vision

We envision a digitally empowered India where no citizen misses out on a government scheme, job opportunity, or essential service due to a lack of awareness or technical difficulty. We aim to be the most trusted independent gateway for accessing Indian civic services online.

Protecting Citizens from Digital Fraud

The internet is filled with confusing websites, misleading information, and unfortunately, fraudulent portals that mimic official government sites to steal sensitive data or charge unnecessary fees.

Form Sahara acts as your digital shield. We do not collect your personal data, nor do we process your applications. Instead, we act as a secure directory. Our team manually verifies every single link provided on this platform to ensure it redirects you only to the official .gov.in or .nic.in portals. By doing this, we save citizens time, money, and the risk of falling prey to cyber fraud.
